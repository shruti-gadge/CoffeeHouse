Coffee & Treats Menu – Perfect Pairings for Every Mood!

Classic Brews

Espresso – A bold and intense shot of pure coffee perfection.
Americano – Smooth espresso with hot water for a rich yet mellow taste.
Macchiato – A shot of espresso "stained" with a touch of frothy milk.
Cortado – A balanced blend of espresso and steamed milk.

Milky & Creamy Indulgences

Cappuccino – A velvety espresso topped with steamed milk and airy foam.
Latte – Smooth espresso mixed with creamy steamed milk for a comforting taste.
Mocha – A chocolate-infused espresso delight with steamed milk.
Flat White – A strong espresso-based drink with a silky, velvety texture.

Cold & Refreshing Specials

Iced Americano – A refreshing cold version of the classic Americano.
Iced Latte – Smooth espresso poured over chilled milk and ice.
Cold Brew – Slow-steeped coffee with a naturally sweet and smooth flavor.
Frappe – A frosty, blended coffee treat with a touch of sweetness.

Signature & Flavored Specialties

Caramel Bliss Latte – Creamy latte infused with caramel drizzle and whipped cream.
Hazelnut Heaven Cappuccino – A nutty twist on a classic cappuccino.
Vanilla Bean Latte – A smooth and aromatic vanilla-infused coffee delight.
Coconut Mocha – Rich mocha with a tropical hint of coconut.

Non-Coffee Indulgences

Matcha Latte – A creamy and earthy green tea delight.
Golden Turmeric Latte – A warm, spiced latte packed with health benefits.
Chai Latte – A comforting blend of black tea, spices, and frothy milk.
Hot Chocolate – A rich, velvety chocolate experience.
