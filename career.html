Join Our Team – Careers at Coffee House

Why Work With Us?

At Coffee House, we’re more than just a coffee shop — we’re a community. We believe in great coffee, excellent customer service, and creating a welcoming space for everyone. If you’re passionate about coffee, love working in a fun and fast-paced environment, and enjoy making people’s day brighter, we’d love to have you on our team!

What We Offer ?

Competitive Pay – Earn a great hourly wage plus tips!
Training & Growth – Learn the art of coffee making and develop customer service skills.
Career Advancement – We promote from within! Many of our baristas become managers.
Fun & Friendly Environment – Work with an energetic and supportive team.
Flexible Schedules – We offer full-time, part-time, and seasonal positions.

Open Positions

Barista

What You'll Do ?

Craft delicious coffee and espresso drinks.

Provide friendly and efficient customer service.

Keep the café clean and stocked.

Work as a team to create a welcoming atmosphere.

What We’re Looking For:

A positive attitude and love for coffee.

Strong communication and teamwork skills.

Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Previous experience is a plus but not required!

Cafe Manager

What You'll Do ?

Lead and inspire a team of baristas.

Oversee daily operations and customer experience.

Manage inventory, scheduling, and quality control.

Ensure a clean, safe, and fun work environment.

What We’re Looking For:

1+ years of leadership experience in food service or hospitality.

Excellent problem-solving and leadership skills.

Passion for coffee and great customer service.


Customer Service & Cashier

What You'll Do ?

Greet and assist customers with orders.

Handle transactions and maintain an organized register.

Ensure customers have a positive experience.

What We’re Looking For:

Friendly and outgoing personality.

Strong multitasking and communication skills.

Ability to work efficiently in a busy environment.
